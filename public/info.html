App Protocol

Operating Manual

01

Identity Verification

Initialize your session by entering your official registration number on the main login screen.

02

Protocol Sequentiality

Scanner strictly enforces sequential movement. You must log each floor in order (1, 2, 3...) to earn points.

03

Rewards Procurement

Accumulated points can be exchanged for premium perks in the "Available Perks" section. Each redemption is immediately logged.
